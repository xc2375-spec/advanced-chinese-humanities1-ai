About the course

Why this course and platform

Course Positioning

Advanced Chinese for Humanities (II) is designed for upper-level Chinese learners. This volume focuses on art, poetry, fiction, women, marriage, and society, helping students engage with more abstract, interpretive, and culturally rich Chinese texts.

What Students Learn

Students learn not only vocabulary and sentence patterns, but also how to discuss aesthetics, literary meaning, personal emotion, social roles, historical change, and cultural values in Chinese.

How AI Helps

This site supports vocabulary discovery, reading comprehension, discussion preparation, fluency practice, and writing development. The goal is to help students move from reading Chinese texts to interpreting ideas and expressing thoughtful responses in Chinese.
