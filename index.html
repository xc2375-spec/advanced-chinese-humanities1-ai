About the course

Why this textbook and platform

Course Positioning

Advanced Chinese for Humanities (I) is designed for upper-level Chinese learners. It supports students in deepening language proficiency while entering a broader world of history, literature, philosophy, art, and cultural traditions.

What Students Learn

The course brings together modern Chinese language with humanistic themes. Students do not only learn vocabulary and sentence patterns, but also explore how Chinese expresses ideas about the universe, society, ethics, culture, and human experience.

How AI Helps

This site provides support for vocabulary discovery, reading comprehension, discussion, fluency practice, and writing. The goal is to help students move gradually from “learning Chinese” to “using Chinese to understand people and the world.”
