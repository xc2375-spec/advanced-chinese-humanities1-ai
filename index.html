About the course

Why this course and platform

Course Positioning

Advanced Chinese for Humanities (I) is designed for upper-level Chinese learners. It supports students in deepening language proficiency while entering a broader world of history, literature, philosophy, art, mythology, and cultural traditions.

What Students Learn

The course combines modern Chinese language study with humanistic themes. Students learn not only vocabulary and sentence patterns, but also ways of expressing ideas about the universe, society, ethics, memory, culture, and human experience.

How AI Helps

This site supports vocabulary discovery, reading comprehension, discussion preparation, fluency practice, and writing development. The goal is to help students move from "learning Chinese" to "using Chinese to understand people and the world."
