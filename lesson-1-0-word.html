Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals and character clues

1. 氵 and water-related meaning

In this lesson, the 氵 radical often suggests water, movement, or an unstable physical state.

混hùnmix
海hǎisea
洋yángocean
浮fúfloat
沉chénsink
Memory tip: when you see 氵, ask whether the character may relate to water, flow, mixing, or change in physical position.
In this lesson, what does the 氵 radical most often help suggest?

2. 礻 and the sacred or ritual world

The component 礻 often appears in characters connected with belief, ritual, blessing, or the sacred.

神shéngod
祝zhùwish / bless
福fúblessing
祈qípray
Memory tip: 礻 can help you remember that a character may belong to the world of religion, ritual language, or sacred meaning.
Why is 礻 a useful clue in the character 神?

3. 文 as a bridge to knowledge and culture

The shared character 文 can connect ideas of writing, patterned knowledge, and civilization.

文wénwriting / culture
天文tiānwénastronomy
文明wénmíngcivilization
文化wénhuàculture
Memory tip: shared characters often help build a memory bridge. 文 can connect several advanced academic words that feel abstract at first.
What does the shared character 文 help connect in 天文 and 文明?

4. Learn contrast pairs together

Some characters are easier to remember when they are learned as a pair. This is especially true when they share a radical and express opposite movement.

浮fúfloat
沉chénsink
阳yángyang
阴yīnyin
Memory tip: if two characters form a meaningful contrast, learning them together often improves recall and reduces confusion.
Why are 浮 and 沉 useful to learn together?
