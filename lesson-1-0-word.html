Lesson 1.0

天·地·人

This opening lesson introduces a vocabulary network around the universe, creation myths, civilization, science, and the human search for origins. Instead of memorizing words one by one, students are encouraged to see how these words connect to larger concepts.

Unit 1: 上下五千年 Theme: Cosmos, Myth, Civilization Module: Word Explorer

Concept groups

Vocabulary as a network

A. 宇宙与起源

How do people explain where the world comes from?

B. 神话与文明

Myths and legends are also ways for civilizations to describe the world.

C. 世界的构成

Words that describe how the world and life take shape.

D. 对立、结合与变化

Pairs and process words help students see contrast, division, and union.

Focus patterns

  • 混 family: 混沌 / 混合 / 混乱
  • 神 family: 神 / 神话
  • Process words: 形成 / 分成 / 结合 / 创造
  • Contrast pair: 浮 / 沉
  • Concept pair: 阳性 / 阴性

Proper nouns in this lesson

盘古 埃及 巴比伦 印度 希腊 圣经 夏娃 女娲

AI-ready directions

Click a word chip below or use the tools section to explore explanation, comparison, sentence building, and discussion support.

  • Explain the difference between 神话 and 传说.
  • Compare 混沌, 混合, and 混乱.
  • Use 形成 and 起源 in two different sentences.
  • Discuss how 神话 and science explain the world differently.

Learning goal

Students should begin to notice that advanced vocabulary is often easier to remember when grouped by concept, structure, and contrast.
