Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals and character clues

1. 辶 and movement or direction

In this lesson, the component 辶 often helps suggest movement, path, or change of direction.

追zhuīchase
迷mílose one’s way
退tuìstep back
选xuǎnchoose
Memory tip: when you see 辶, ask whether the character may involve movement, route, pursuit, retreat, or going in a certain direction.
In this lesson, what does 辶 most clearly help suggest in 追, 迷, and 退?

2. 扌 and direct human action

The hand radical 扌 often helps indicate handling, striking, directing, or control through action.

打dǎhit / fight
指zhǐpoint
控kòngcontrol
推tuīpush
Memory tip: 扌 often signals an action done by the hand or directed by human control. This is useful for remembering words like 打仗 and 控制.
Why is 扌 a useful clue in characters such as 打 and 控?

3. 病 and the illness network

This lesson includes a cluster of words about medicine and treatment: 医术, 神医, and 治病.

病bìngillness
医yīmedical
疗liáotreat
疾jídisease
Memory tip: when you meet words around 病, try to build a medical network: illness, treatment, doctor, and healing skill.
In 治病, what is the most important clue carried by 病?

4. 讠 and preserving knowledge in words

In this lesson, words such as 记忆, 歌诀, and 传 show how language helps preserve historical memory.

记jìrecord / remember
诀juéformula / mnemonic
讲jiǎngspeak / explain
说shuōspeak / say
Memory tip: 讠 often points to speech, wording, formula, or recorded language. That makes it useful for remembering how knowledge is transmitted.
What does 讠 most often suggest in characters such as 记 and 诀?
