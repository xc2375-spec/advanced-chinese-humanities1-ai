Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals and shared characters

1. 礻 / 示 and ritual meaning

In this lesson, 礼 and 祭 belong to a semantic field of ritual, ceremony, and formal respect.

礼lǐritual / etiquette
祭jìoffer sacrifice
福fúblessing
祝zhùbless / wish
Memory tip: when you see 礻 or 示-related forms, ask whether the character may connect to ritual, respect, blessing, or the sacred.
What does 礻 / 示 most usefully suggest in 礼 and 祭?

2. 学 as a bridge across academic words

The shared character 学 helps connect doctrine, scholar, and school of thought.

学说xuéshuōdoctrine
学者xuézhěscholar
学派xuépàischool of thought
学生xuéshēngstudent
Memory tip: shared characters often make advanced vocabulary easier to remember. Here, 学 helps build a learning network across several abstract words.
What does the shared character 学 help connect in 学说, 学者, and 学派?

3. 实 and making ideas real

In this lesson, 实际, 实行, and 实现 all relate to what is real, practical, or carried out.

实际shíjìactual
实行shíxíngcarry out
实现shíxiànrealize
真实zhēnshíreal
Memory tip: when you notice 实 in several words, ask whether the idea has to do with reality, practice, or making something concrete.
What is the strongest shared idea in 实际, 实行, and 实现?

4. 扌 and direct physical action

The hand radical 扌 often points to handling, moving, or physically doing something.

搬bānmove
摆bǎiplace / display
推tuīpush
拿nátake
Memory tip: 扌 often suggests an action carried out by the hand or through direct physical manipulation.
Why is 扌 a useful clue in characters such as 搬 and 摆?
