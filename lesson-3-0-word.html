Lesson 3.0

琴棋书画

Focus on Patterns

Learn through 心, 忄, and the brush-and-ink system

1. 心 as the center of mind, inner state, and cultivation

In this lesson, 心 is not just a body part. It points to mind, inner condition, attention, calmness, and self-cultivation.

心xīnheart / mind
心智xīnzhìmind
心如止水xīn rú zhǐ shuǐstill mind
静心jìngxīncalm the mind
入迷rùmíbe engrossed
修身xiūshēncultivate oneself
Memory tip: when you see 心 in this lesson, ask whether the word is about thought, concentration, emotional state, or inner cultivation. This helps connect 心智, 心如止水, 静心养性, and even words like 入迷.
What best connects 心智, 心如止水, and 静心养性 in this lesson?

2. 忄 often suggests emotion, temperament, or inner condition

The component 忄 often appears in characters related to emotion, temperament, or mental condition. In this lesson, it is a useful clue in words such as 惊慌 and in the character 性 inside 静心养性.

惊jīngin 惊慌
慌huāngin 惊慌
性xìngin 养性
快kuàimood / speed
Memory tip: 忄 is a compressed form related to 心. When you see 忄, ask whether the character is connected with feeling, reaction, or inner state. That helps with 惊慌 and 养性.
Why is 忄 a helpful clue in words like 惊慌 and 养性?

3. 笔墨系统: learn writing as a sequence

Traditional writing and painting vocabulary becomes easier when learned as a sequence: tool → action → model → imitation → cultivated skill.

墨mòink
砚yàninkstone
提笔tí bǐtake up pen
下笔xiàbǐstart writing
临摹línmócopy from model
模仿mófǎngimitate
技艺jìyìskill
Memory tip: if students memorize writing words as isolated items, they are easy to forget. If they memorize them as a chain—tool, start, model, imitation, skill—the whole group becomes much easier.
Why are 墨, 砚, 提笔, 下笔, 临摹, and 模仿 useful to learn together?

4. From aesthetic life to moral cultivation

This lesson is not only about art objects. It moves from artistic taste to inner balance, from appreciation to self-cultivation.

审美shěnměiaesthetic judgment
平衡pínghéngbalance
和谐héxiéharmony
精髓jīngsuǐessence
修身xiūshēnself-cultivation
素养sùyǎngcultivation
Memory tip: in this lesson, the arts are not treated as decoration only. They are linked to inner poise, judgment, balance, and character. That larger idea helps students remember the vocabulary more meaningfully.

Usage Highlights

Example-based learning

掌握 · 讲究 · 修身

只有认真复习，才能真正掌握新的生词和课文。
写文章一定要讲究逻辑，也要讲究表达。
孔子认为，通过修身可以成为更好的人。

心如止水 · 惊慌 · 入迷

在复杂的工作中还能心如止水，是很难得的能力。
遇到危险最好不要惊慌，要先平静下来。
他玩游戏玩得很入迷，没有听见你讲的话。

下笔 · 提笔 · 临摹

下笔之前先想清楚结构，写出来会更有条理。
提笔时要先定气，心里不要太乱。
写好汉字的一个好方法，就是临摹书法家的字。

审美 · 平衡 · 和谐

不同地方、不同年代的人，审美标准可能不一样。
他们常常讨论如何平衡工作和生活的关系。
新的博物馆设计体现了传统与发展之间的和谐。
