Lesson 3.1

王羲之换鹅

This lesson connects calligraphy, gesture, practice, talent, and public recognition. Try to learn the new words through patterns: repeated hand actions, effort and strength, and character-building logic such as 众.

Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals, parts, and character-building logic

1. 牛字旁：从 “特” 进去记

In this lesson, the easiest entry point for the 牛字旁 / 牜 pattern is the character 特 in 特殊 and 特地. Historically, 牜 comes from 牛. In modern words, the semantic link is not always transparent, but noticing the form still helps memory.

特tèspecial
特殊tèshūspecial
特地tèdìespecially
物wùthing
牧mùto herd
Memory tip: 牛字旁 is useful not because every word still clearly means “cow,” but because it helps students notice recurring shape families. In this lesson, start from 特 → 特殊 / 特地, then expand outward.
In this lesson, which word is the best anchor for noticing the 牛字旁 pattern?

2. 提手旁：手的动作特别多

The component 扌 often signals hand action. This lesson is especially good for this pattern because many important verbs involve writing, asking, touching, or moving with the hand.

打dǎto do / hit
打听dǎtingto inquire
抄chāocopy by hand
拍pāito clap
把bǎto hold
Memory tip: when students see 扌, ask: “Is this a hand action?” In this lesson, 打听, 抄写, and 拍 all become easier to remember that way.
Why are 打听, 抄, and 拍 useful to learn together?

3. 部件 “力”：力气、干劲、功劳

The part 力 often points toward strength, effort, labor, or result. This lesson gives a very teachable group around work, drive, and recognized contribution.

力lìstrength
劲jìndrive
干劲gànjìnvigor
功gōngachievement
劳láolabor
功劳gōngláocredit, merit
眼力yǎnlìjudgment
Memory tip: 力 can help students build a semantic bridge: physical strength → drive → effort → achievement → credit. That makes 干劲, 功劳, and 眼力 feel less isolated.
In this lesson, what is the most useful meaning clue carried by 力?

4. 三个一样的部件：从 众 走向 品、晶、森

The character 众 is a great teaching point because it is built from repeated human figures. In this lesson, it appears in 与众不同 and 出众. From there, students can expand to other “tripled” characters.

众zhòngmany people
与众不同yǔ zhòng bù tóngdistinctive
出众chūzhòngstand out
品pǐngrade /品味
晶jīngcrystal / bright
森sēnforest
Memory tip: repeated-component characters are visually memorable. Teach students to notice the logic: three 人 → 众, three 木 → 森, three 日 → 晶. In this lesson, 众 is the anchor because it directly feeds two useful advanced words.
