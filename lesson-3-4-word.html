Lesson 3.4

成语故事二则

This lesson brings together two vivid idiom stories: 天衣无缝 and 胸有成竹. Students can learn the words through radicals, image chains, and story-based memory: body movement, door-frame characters, 酉-related words, and idiom logic.

A. 天衣无缝：天上、人间、完美无缺

These words help students enter the first idiom story through celestial imagery and the idea of flawlessness.

the key image in 天衣无缝: no seam means no visible flaw

Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals, frames, and idiom memory

1. 身字旁：身体移动、躲闪、姿态变化

In this lesson, the best entry point for 身字旁 is 躲 in 躲雨. This radical often helps students think about body movement, posture, or how the body shifts in space.

躲duǒto hide / dodge
躲雨duǒyǔtake shelter from rain
躺tǎngto lie down
躯qūbody / torso
身shēnbody
Memory tip: teach 身字旁 through action and body image: the body bends, shifts, dodges, or takes shelter. In this lesson, 躲雨 is the best anchor because students can immediately picture the action.
In Lesson 3.4, what is the most useful learning clue carried by 身字旁?

2. 门字框：门里门外、听见、看见、闪现

The frame 门 often helps students imagine a doorway, an enclosed opening, or the idea of something appearing through a frame. In this lesson, 闻名 and 闪 in 电闪雷鸣 are very teachable anchors.

闻wénto hear
闻名wénmíngwell-known
闪shǎnflash
电闪雷鸣diànshǎn léimíngthunder and lightning
间jiānbetween / room
Memory tip: teach 门字框 with visual framing: something is heard through the doorway, flashes at the doorway, or exists in a space defined by a frame. That makes 闻名 and 闪 much easier to remember together.
In Lesson 3.4, why are 闻名 and 电闪雷鸣 useful to learn together?

3. 酉字旁：从“酷”“配”进去，不只和酒有关

The radical 酉 historically connects with wine and fermentation, but in modern Chinese it often appears in words that no longer transparently mean “alcohol.” In this lesson, the best anchors are 酷暑 and 配合.

酷kùextreme / cool
酷暑kùshǔsultry summer
配pèimatch / coordinate
配合pèihécoordinate
酒jiǔwine
Memory tip: tell students not to over-literalize 酉字旁. Sometimes it helps by shape-family recognition more than by exact modern meaning. In this lesson, 酷 and 配 are the most practical anchors.
What is the best way to teach 酉字旁 in Lesson 3.4?

4. 成语怎么记：故事 + 画面 + 核心义

This lesson is ideal for teaching idiom memory because the idioms are highly visual. Students remember them best when they connect the story, the mental image, and the modern figurative meaning.

天衣无缝no seam in heavenly clothesperfect, flawless
胸有成竹bamboo already in chesthave a clear plan
栩栩如生vivid as alivelifelike
电闪雷鸣lightning + thunderstorm image
倾盆大雨water pouring from a basintorrential rain
Memory tip: use a three-step method: 1) see the picture, 2) remember the story, 3) map it to the modern meaning. For example, 天衣无缝 is not only about heavenly clothes—it becomes “perfect and flawless.” 胸有成竹 is not only about bamboo—it becomes “having a plan before acting.”
