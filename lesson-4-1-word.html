Lesson 4.1

梁山伯与祝英台

This lesson connects legend, folk transmission, education, talent, arranged marriage, loyalty, separation, and tragic love. Try to learn the new words through visible character patterns: characters with the 口 component, top-bottom structures, 土-bottom forms, 王-bottom forms, and 言-related forms.

Focus on Patterns

Learn through parts, structures, and visible character families

1. 含口字部件的字：说、答、合、名、喻

This lesson gives several useful words containing the 口 component, which often helps students think about speech, response, naming, or an opening/closing action.

口头kǒutóuoral
答应dāyingto agree / respond
合上héshangto close
名师míngshīgreat teacher
家喻户晓yù / xiǎoknown to all
Memory tip: when students see 口 as a component, ask whether the word involves saying, responding, naming, calling, or an opening/closing movement. In this lesson, 口头 / 答应 / 合上 / 名师 make a nice starter cluster.

2. 上下结构：看“学、答、委、墓、虹”这些字怎么叠起来

This lesson is rich in top-bottom structure. Students can strengthen visual organization by noticing that many important characters are built vertically rather than left-right.

学xuétop-bottom
答dátop-bottom
委wěitop-bottom
墓mùtop-bottom
虹hóngleft-right actually, contrast
英yīngtop-bottom
Memory tip: do not only teach meaning—teach shape habits. In this lesson, students can compare top-bottom words like 学 / 答 / 委 / 墓 / 英 with non-top-bottom words to sharpen visual awareness.

3. 土字底、王字底：墓、墓地；宝、英台里的“王”形收尾

This lesson offers a good chance to point out 土-bottom and also some visually memorable lower parts that students can notice as “ending shapes,” including 王-like bottoms in certain characters.

墓mù土 at the bottom
坟墓fénmùtomb
台táibase / platform shape
宝bǎobottom shape cue
英yīngclear lower ending
Memory tip: for intermediate learners, it is often enough to say: “Look at the bottom ending shape.” That makes characters like 墓 easier to remember because students can anchor the lower part visually. Here, 墓 is the strongest 土字底 example in the lesson.
In Lesson 4.1, which word is the clearest 土字底 teaching anchor?

4. 言字底 / 言字旁相关：许、答应、口头、发誓

This lesson is also strong for speech and promise language. Even when the exact form is not always the same, students can group together words tied to speaking, promising, swearing, and verbal agreement.

许xǔpermit / promise
允许yǔnxǔto permit
答应dāyingto agree
发誓fāshìto vow
口头kǒutóuoral
许配xǔpèito betroth
Memory tip: here the best teaching move is to build a speech-act network: speaking, agreeing, promising, permitting, vowing. That makes 允许 / 答应 / 发誓 / 口头 / 许配 easier to remember together.
