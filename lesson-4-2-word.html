Lesson 4.2

武则天

This lesson focuses on China's only female emperor, Wu Zetian (624–705), exploring her political achievements, historical controversies, and legacy. Learn the new words through character patterns: 虫字旁, 含有寸的部件, 隹部件, and the characters 于／予／矛.

Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals, semantic fields, and character families

1. 虫字旁：飞蛾与生命力

This lesson features 飞蛾, giving students an entry into 虫字旁. This radical appears in many words related to insects and small creatures, and carries a sense of fragility, instinct, or natural drive—apt for discussing the moth-to-flame metaphor in the text.

蛾émoth
飞蛾fēi'émoth
虫chónginsect
蝴蝶húdiébutterfly
蜜蜂mìfēngbee
蚂蚁mǎyǐant
Memory tip: 虫字旁 is a visual clue for insects and small creatures. In this lesson, 飞蛾 (moth) appears in the idiom 自取灭亡 context—a moth rushing toward flame— making this radical especially memorable and thematically rich.

2. 含有寸的部件：衬量与法度

Several words in this lesson contain the component 寸, which originally measured a small unit of length and carries connotations of precision, measurement, and control. It appears in words related to managing, holding, and guiding.

寸cùnunit / tiny
持chíto hold / maintain
对duìcorrect / toward
寻xúnto seek
导dǎoto guide
尊zūnto respect
Memory tip: 寸 is a subtle but recurring component. In this lesson, 持 (to hold, to maintain) is the most useful anchor. Teach students to look for 寸 in words about controlling, measuring, or keeping—ideas very relevant to Wu Zetian's story.
Which lesson word best illustrates the 寸 component and its meaning of holding or maintaining?

3. 隹部件：鸟形字与传达

The component 隹 (short-tailed bird) appears in several characters in this lesson. It carries a visual sense of birds and flight, and often shows up in words about communication, transmission, or passage—perfect for discussing Wu Zetian's far-reaching influence.

隹zhuīshort-tailed bird
雁yànwild goose
难nándifficult
确quèdefinite / sure
谁shuíwho
推tuīto push
Memory tip: 隹 is an ancient bird pictograph. In this lesson, 雁 (wild goose) is the most visually striking anchor. The idiom 树碑立传 relates to leaving a lasting mark—like a goose leaving traces. Use 难 and 确 to extend the pattern into more common everyday words.
Which of the following words in Lesson 4.2 contains the 隹 component?

4. 于／予／矛：形近字辨析

This lesson features 善于、给予 and the idiom 敢作敢为, making it an ideal opportunity to distinguish the look-alike characters 于、予、矛. Students often confuse these; careful visual comparison here is very valuable.

于yúin / at / to (prep.)
善于shànyúto be adept at
予yǔto give / I (classical)
给予jǐyǔto give / to accord
矛máospear / contradiction
矛盾máodùncontradiction
Memory tip: 于 has a top horizontal stroke and is a preposition meaning "in/at/to." 予 has a downward hook and means "to give." 矛 is a spear shape with a dot at the top—it means spear and appears in 矛盾. Use 善于 / 给予 / 矛盾 as the three anchors for this visual trio.
Which pair correctly matches the character to its meaning?
