Focus on Patterns

Learn through radicals, semantic fields, and character families

1. 单人旁：人与人之间的行为

This lesson is rich in 单人旁 (亻) characters. The radical signals words related to human action, social behavior, and interpersonal conduct—perfectly suited to a lesson about how men and women interact.

亻rénperson
例子lìziexample
优美yōuměigraceful
偷懒tōulǎnto be lazy
做zuòto do
依赖yīlàito depend on
Memory tip: 单人旁 is one of the most productive radicals in Chinese. In this lesson, 例子 and 优美 are good anchors. Teach students that 亻 often marks words about how people act, behave, or relate to others— ideas at the core of this lesson's gender theme.
In Lesson 4.3, what is the best way to use 单人旁 (亻) as a teaching tool?

2. 提手旁：动手、控制、给予

Several action verbs in this lesson contain 提手旁 (扌). This radical signals hands-on action: grabbing, pulling, waving, resisting, rescuing. In a lesson about conflict and relationships, these words carry strong physical energy.

扌shǒuhand
撕扯sīchěto tear or pull
挽救wǎnjiùto save, rescue
抗拒kàngjùto resist
执掌zhízhǎngto wield power
接近jiējìnto get close to
Memory tip: 提手旁 (扌) is the hand in motion. In this lesson, 撕扯 (to tear apart) is the most vivid anchor—both literally and as a metaphor for a strained relationship. Extend to 挽救 / 抗拒 / 执掌 to build a network of action verbs rooted in physical force and control.
Which group of words from Lesson 4.3 best illustrates 提手旁 (扌)?

3. 带火字部件：热度、情绪与能量

Several words in this lesson contain a 火 (fire) component—either as 火 itself or as the four-dot variant 灬. These characters often carry connotations of heat, intensity, or strong emotion, fitting for a lesson full of passion and conflict.

火huǒfire
发挥fāhuīto bring into play
烦恼fánnǎoworry, trouble
热情rèqíngenthusiasm
炒chǎoto stir-fry / to fire sb.
灿烂cànlànbrilliant, radiant
Memory tip: The fire component appears in two forms: 火 (left or bottom) and 灬 (four dots at the bottom, as in 热). In this lesson, 发挥 is the key anchor — the 挥 contains the fire component via its root energy. Extend to 烦恼 / 热情 to show how fire metaphorically fuels emotion and intensity.
What do characters with the fire component (火 / 灬) often share in meaning?

4. 力字部件：力量、努力与劳动

The component 力 (force, strength) appears in several words in this lesson. It marks concepts of effort, power, capacity, and labor—themes directly relevant to how gender roles and social power are discussed in this text.

力lìforce / strength
勤劳qínláohardworking
努力nǔlìto work hard
能力nénglìability, capability
劳动láodònglabor, work
助zhùto help
Memory tip: 力 as a component signals effort and capability. In this lesson, 勤劳 (hardworking) is the strongest anchor — 劳 contains 力 at the bottom. Extend to 努力 / 能力 / 劳动 to build a rich semantic field around work, labor, and human capacity that resonates with the lesson's gendered division of labor theme.
