Lesson 4.4

职场女性

This lesson examines the challenges and opportunities facing working women in contemporary China, covering employment, workplace discrimination, maternity, feminism, and identity. Learn the new words through character patterns: 衣补旁, 三点水 vs. 两点水, 国字框, and 耳刀旁.

1. 衣补旁（衤）：布料、衣物与补助

The 衣补旁 (衤)—the clothing radical—appears in several words in this lesson. Originally related to garments and fabric, it extends to words about covering, supplementing, or compensating—all directly relevant to welfare and policy vocabulary.

衤yīclothing
补贴bǔtiēsubsidy
福利fúlìwelfare, benefits
衬衫chènshānshirt
裤子kùzitrousers
初chūinitial, first
Memory tip: 衤 looks like 衣 with the bottom split off—it is the left-side form of the clothing radical. In this lesson, 补贴 is the best anchor: 补 means to mend or supplement (fix like a patch on cloth), and 贴 means to paste or stick. Together they describe a financial subsidy—like patching a gap.

2. 三点水 vs. 两点水：形近辨析

This lesson gives a good opportunity to contrast 三点水 (氵) and 两点水 (冫). Both look like drops of water, but they come from different origins: 氵 relates to water and liquid; 冫 relates to ice and coldness. The contrast sharpens students' visual precision.

氵shuǐwater (3 dots)
流产liúchǎnmiscarriage
泡影pàoyǐngsoap bubble / illusion
冫bīngice (2 dots)
准zhǔnstandard / to allow
批准pīzhǔnto approve
Memory tip: Three drops = flowing water (氵): 流, 泡, 洗, 游. Two drops = frozen water (冫): 冷, 冰, 准, 冻. In this lesson, 流产 anchors 氵 and 批准 anchors 冫. Ask students: is this word about something fluid and moving, or something cold and fixed?
Which pair correctly matches each water radical to a Lesson 4.4 word?

3. 国字框（囗）：围合、范围与限制

The 国字框 (囗) is an enclosing frame that surrounds a character's interior. It visually represents enclosure, boundary, or containment—fitting for a lesson about institutional structures and the boxes that constrain women's careers.

囗wéienclosure
困惑kùnhuòperplexed
国guócountry
固定gùdìngfixed, regular
图túdiagram, plan
围wéito surround
Memory tip: 囗 is a box—it encloses what is inside. In this lesson, 困惑 is the most vivid anchor: 困 shows a tree (木) trapped inside a box—the perfect image for feeling stuck or hemmed in. Use this to discuss how career women can feel confined by social expectations and workplace structures.
Why is 困 such a visually effective character for teaching 国字框?

4. 耳刀旁（阝）：地域、组织与阶层

The 耳刀旁 (阝) appears in two positions: on the left it derives from 阜 (mound, hill) and relates to terrain and hierarchy; on the right it derives from 邑 (settlement) and relates to place and administrative units. Both senses are relevant to this lesson's institutional vocabulary.

阝(左)fùmound / hierarchy
阶jiērank / step
阝(右)yìsettlement / place
部bùsection / ministry
队duìteam / group
都dōu / dūall; capital city
Memory tip: Left-side 阝 relates to elevation and structure (阶级, 阻碍, 防); right-side 阝 relates to place and organization (部门, 队伍, 郊区). In this lesson, 部门 (department/ministry) and 队 (team) are useful anchors. Teach students: is the 阝 on the left or right? That tells you which meaning family it belongs to.
When 阝 appears on the LEFT side of a character, what meaning family does it typically belong to?
