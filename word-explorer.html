Word Explorer

Build vocabulary as a system, not as isolated lists.

This section helps students explore vocabulary through semantic groups, character families, contrast pairs, word formation, and key humanistic concepts. The goal is not only to memorize words, but also to discover patterns, relationships, and ways of thinking through language.

Unit 1

上下五千年 · Word Explorer

1.0 天·地·人

Explore vocabulary about the universe, mythology, civilization, creation, science, and the origin of humanity.

1.1 黄帝的传说

Learn words related to tribes, rulers, war, invention, memory, cultural origins, and early civilization.

1.2 孔子

Build vocabulary around thought, ritual, teaching, virtue, argument, harmony, and schools of thought.

1.3 司马迁和《史记》

Explore language about writing, records, accomplishment, punishment, integrity, historical memory, and biography.

1.4 成语故事二则

Study idiom stories through figurative meaning, oral use, character action, consequence, and moral interpretation.

Unit 2

人文天下 · Word Explorer

2.0 人与世界

Explore vocabulary about dreams, reality, philosophy, time, life, destiny, and the human search for meaning.

2.1 我

Build a system of words related to selfhood, body and soul, identity, contrast, reality, and inward reflection.

2.2 寻梦

Learn vocabulary around searching, loss, struggle, encouragement, uncertainty, perseverance, and aspiration.

2.3 聪明人和傻子和奴才

Explore social roles, suffering, injustice, sympathy, judgment, and the language of critique.

2.4 塞翁失马

Study words about fortune, misfortune, judgment, reversal, foresight, and the wisdom of uncertainty.

Unit 3

艺术人生 · Word Explorer

3.0 琴棋书画

Explore vocabulary about traditional arts, cultivation, aesthetic taste, cultural accomplishment, and artistic life.

3.1 王羲之换鹅

Learn words related to calligraphy, exchange, admiration, artistic skill, reputation, and cultural anecdote.

3.2 曹植的《七步诗》

Build vocabulary around poetry, talent, rivalry, emotion, historical tension, and literary expression.

3.3 曹雪芹和《红楼梦》

Explore language about fiction, character, love, decline, memory, and the world of classical literature.

3.4 成语故事二则

Study idioms through imagery, figurative meaning, oral use, and the relationship between story and interpretation.

Unit 4

4.2 武则天

4.3 女人

Explore language about womanhood, identity, emotion, expectation, social life, and personal voice.

Study words related to pressure, contradiction, choice, work, family, and modern social concerns.

Recommended starting points
